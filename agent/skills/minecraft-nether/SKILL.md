## Strategy

1. Gather 10 obsidian using diamond_pickaxe: `mine` obsidian count=10.
2. Find a flat area near your base.
3. Build the portal frame: 4 wide x 5 tall obsidian frame (corners can be any block).
   - Place 4 obsidian on the ground in a row.
   - Stack 3 obsidian on each side.
   - Place 4 obsidian across the top.
4. Light the portal with flint_and_steel: `craft` flint_and_steel (1 iron_ingot + 1 flint).
   - Get flint by mining gravel: `mine` gravel.
5. `use_item` while looking at the portal frame to light it.
6. Walk into the purple portal to enter the Nether.

## Tips

- Bring: iron/diamond sword, iron armor, shield, bow + arrows, food, cobblestone (64+), crafting table.
- Cobblestone is essential in the Nether — ghast fireballs can't destroy it.
- The Nether is extremely dangerous. Ghasts shoot fireballs, blazes shoot fire charges, piglins attack on sight unless you wear gold armor.
- Build a cobblestone shelter around your Nether-side portal immediately.
- Bring a crafting table — you'll need to craft in the Nether.

## Lessons Learned

- Gold boots prevent piglin aggro. Craft and equip one gold boot.
- Mark your portal coordinates — getting lost in the Nether is deadly.
