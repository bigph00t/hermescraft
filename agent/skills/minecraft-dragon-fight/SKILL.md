## Strategy

1. Throw eye_of_ender with `use_item` — follow direction it flies.
2. `navigate` in that direction ~200 blocks. Throw again. Repeat until it goes down.
3. When the eye goes down, the stronghold is directly below. Dig down to find it.
4. Find the End portal room inside the stronghold (stone brick structure underground).
5. Fill the portal frame with Eyes of Ender: `place` eye_of_ender on each frame block.
   - The frame has 12 slots. Some may already have eyes. Fill the empty ones.
6. Jump into the portal to enter The End.

### The Dragon Fight
7. The dragon flies around obsidian pillars with end crystals on top.
8. DESTROY THE END CRYSTALS FIRST — they heal the dragon. Shoot with bow or climb and break.
9. When all crystals are destroyed, the dragon will land on the fountain in the center.
10. Sprint to the fountain and attack the dragon with your sword while it's perched.
11. Dodge the dragon's charge attacks and acid breath.
12. Repeat: wait for perch → attack → dodge. Takes several cycles.

## Tips

- Bring: diamond sword, bow + 64 arrows, iron/diamond armor, shield, 64 cobblestone, golden apples, cooked food, ender pearls (for teleporting), water bucket.
- Ender pearls let you teleport to crystal-topped pillars. Throw up at the crystal.
- The void below The End means falling = instant death. Stay on the island.
- Beds EXPLODE in The End — do NOT use them (unless as a weapon, which is advanced).
- The dragon's acid breath creates lingering damage pools. Move away immediately.
- After the dragon dies, a portal home appears at the center fountain. Jump in.

## Lessons Learned

- Destroy ALL end crystals before engaging the dragon. It heals too fast otherwise.
- Bring a pumpkin head to avoid enderman aggro in The End.
