## Strategy

1. From your Nether portal, travel along the X axis (positive or negative) to find a fortress.
   - Fortresses generate in rows along the Z axis, spaced ~200-400 blocks apart on X.
   - `navigate` to X+200, Y=50, Z=current to search.
2. Look for dark brick structures (nether_brick). Fortresses are large — hard to miss.
3. Find a blaze spawner inside the fortress. Blazes float, shoot fire charges.
4. Equip your best sword and shield.
5. Use `attack` target "blaze" — fight one at a time. Block fire charges with shield.
6. Collect 7+ blaze_rod drops. Each blaze has a chance to drop 0-1 rod.
7. You may need to kill 15-20 blazes to get 7 rods.

## Tips

- Blazes only spawn near blaze spawners in fortresses. Don't waste time elsewhere.
- Shield blocks blaze fire charges. Keep it in offhand.
- Build a small cobblestone shelter near the spawner to retreat and eat.
- Wither skeletons also spawn in fortresses — they inflict Wither effect. Avoid or kill.
- If you die in the Nether, items despawn in 5 minutes. Rush back.
- Blaze rods are needed for: Eyes of Ender (blaze_rod → 2 blaze_powder + ender_pearl = eye_of_ender).

## Lessons Learned

- Bring lots of food. Fighting blazes drains hunger fast.
- A bow with arrows is very effective against blazes at range.
